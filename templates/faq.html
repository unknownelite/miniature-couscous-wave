Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is Golden Crypto Investment?
Golden Crypto Investment is an online platform where users invest funds that are locked for a 28-day period. Your investment is used to trade in the cryptocurrency market, and you earn daily interest by logging in every 24 hours to claim your rewards.
2. How Does the Investment Work?
- Investment Lock: Funds are locked for 28 days (no withdrawals before maturity).
- Daily Interest: You must log in every 24 hours to claim your daily interest.
- Compounding: Unclaimed interest is forfeited after 24 hours.
- Payout: At the end of the 28-day term, your initial investment + accumulated interest is released.
3. Why Do I Need to Log In Daily?
To prevent abuse and ensure active participation, daily login is required to claim your interest. If you miss a day, you lose that day's interest.
4. How Is My Investment Used?
Your funds are pooled and traded by our expert crypto traders in:
- Spot trading (buying low, selling high)
- Arbitrage opportunities (exploiting price differences across exchanges)
- Staking/Lending (earning passive yields on stablecoins)
5. What Are the Risks?
- Market Volatility: Crypto prices fluctuate; profits aren't guaranteed.
- Locked Funds: No early withdrawals during the 28-day term.
- Daily Claim Requirement: Missed logins = lost interest.
6. How Much Interest Can I Earn?
Interest rates vary based on:
- Market performance
- Your investment tier (e.g., higher deposits = better rates)

Example rates:
- Basic Tier (≤ $1,000): ~1% daily
- Premium Tier (> $1,000): ~1.5% daily
(Check the platform for current rates)
7. Can I Withdraw Early?
No. The 28-day lockup is strict to ensure trading strategies execute properly.
8. How Do I Get Paid After 28 Days?
On maturity:
1. Your initial investment + interest + mining point is sent to your wallet
2. You can reinvest or withdraw to an external wallet
9. Is My Money Safe?
We prioritize security with:
- Cold Storage (90% of funds offline)
- Smart Contract Audits (if applicable)
However, crypto investments are inherently risky—never invest more than you can afford to lose.
10. What Happens If I Forget to Log In?
- Missed Interest: Unclaimed daily rewards are forfeited
- Investment Safety: Your locked principal remains unaffected
11. Are There Fees?
- Deposit Fees: None (but blockchain gas fees may apply)
- Withdrawal Fees: 1% (after the 28-day term)
12. How Do I Start?
1. Sign up and complete KYC (if required)
2. Deposit crypto (BTC, ETH, USDT, etc.)
3. Log in daily to claim interest
4. Withdraw or reinvest after 28 days
13. Who Can Invest?
- Users aged 18+
- Must comply with local crypto regulations
14. What If the Platform Shuts Down?
- Transparency: We provide monthly trading reports
- Emergency Fund: A portion of profits is reserved for payouts
No platform is risk-free—diversify your investments.
